Membership

Private Club Homewood, Alabama

Pinhigh Golf Society offers a spirit of friendship and community that makes everyone feel welcome.  As a private indoor club, we are open to individual and corporate memberships.  Whether a recreational golfer or a tournament player, you'll love to call Pinhigh Golf Society home.  Many of our members live in the area and have club memberships at various local courses. But everyone agrees, no matter how much time you play at your traditional clubs, you always feel at home at the Pinhigh Golf Society.  And at the end of the day, our members can relax and enjoy the comforts of our indoor club where both relationships and golf are bettered.

The Pinhigh Golf Society provides an exceptional experience for members and their guests.  With outstanding amenities and the opportunity to meet new players at your skill level and make new friends.  Have fun while playing in an air-conditioned state-of-the-art sports facility.  You'll feel what makes us special the moment you walk in the door.  All skill levels and members 19 and older welcomed.

The Pinhigh Golf Society has adopted the following rules of conduct for all members. These rules are applicable to all members and guests. They are meant to insure that all members and guests achieve the maximum enjoyment from the use of the club’s amenities. Violations of these rules will subject the member to disciplinary action as determined to be appropriate by the disciplinary committee or management.

General Conduct and Rules Enforcement 

  • The Society’s Professional Staff has the authority to implement and execute relating to all aspects of the golf simulators, including but not limited to starting times, groupings, enforcement of policies and rules of conduct. All members and guests shall be courteous to all staff, members, and guests. 

Registration and Commencement of Play 

The Rules listed below have been established to assist in fair and orderly access to starting times and to provide for an appropriate pace of of reservation times. 

  • General Reservations for Bay Reservations are available Monday through Saturday beginning at 8am and ending play by 9pm. Sunday Tee Times are available beginning at 10am and ending play by 6pm. Play is based upon a first come, first served reservation system.
  • All members must sign in at the welcome desk before playing in their reserved simulator bay. 
  • One bay may be reserved per member, per session time, with no two or more bays to be reserved at the same period of time without additional fees.  
  • Limit of 4 players per bay reservation unless approved by the Pinhigh Golf Society staff. 
  • Any individual or group who happens to not reserve a bay is allowed to play as a first come, first served basis until the next reserved player(s) time.
  • All bay use requires a Check-in by the member at the time of play. 

Guest Play 

  • All guests must be checked-in by a member. Guests may pay their own fees via credit card or charge member statement, if approved by the member. The member must be present with the guest at all times. 
  • Members may have up to 3 guests at a time. 
  • Members can bring individual guests up to 3 times per month per individual. 
  • A guest may only play up to 24 times total in a calendar year, regardless if they play with different members. 
  • Guests may only use the simulator bays and practice areas if they are with a member and they may pay a $40 guest fee for the day. 

Junior Play 

  • Junior golfers (less than 19 years of age) must be accompanied by an adult member and may not play after 4:30pm on weekdays. Weekends allow junior golfers (age 12 and above) to play accompanied by an adult member.
  • Exceptions to junior play may be allowed by Pinhigh Golf Society Staff approval. 

General Policies 

  • Dogs or any other animals are not allowed on Club property. 
  • Golf club throwing, rude or obnoxious behavior, abuse of other members, guests or staff, or any other similar misconduct is not permitted anywhere on Club property. 
  • If you observe a member violating any rules while on property, please contact the Pinhigh Golf Society Staff and advise the person in charge of the issue to be addressed. Do not confront another member yourself, ask a club staff member to do it. 
  • Any member breaking or damaging or removing any property of the Club from the Club premises will be asked to pay the cost thereof, or to replace it. 
  • Smoking is prohibited inside Pinhigh Golf Society.
